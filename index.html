horse402 : On-Chain Horse Racing

We host decentralized horse races for $horse402 token holders — all runs, payouts, and rewards are executed through our native 402 Protocol logic, built on Jito for fast and fair Solana transactions.
10% of total supply is distributed as the global Prize Pool across scheduled Games.

  • Eligibility: hold 100,000+ $horse402 to enter. Your wallet automatically appears on the leaderboard and receives a horse assignment.
  • Format: each Game runs in waves — Round 1: 40 heats × 10 Round 2: 4 heats × 10 Final: 4. All heats within a round start in parallel — you can open any and watch live.
  • Payouts: every Game allocates a share of the on-chain Prize Pool; the 402 Protocol handles distribution automatically — Finals pay the highest rewards.
